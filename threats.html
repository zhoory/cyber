Cybersecurity Threats

  • Phishing

    Fake emails or websites that trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card numbers.

    Learn more about Phishing

  • Malware

    Malicious software such as viruses, worms, or trojans that can damage or control systems without user consent.

    Learn more about Malware

  • Ransomware

    Encrypts data and demands payment for decryption. Often spreads via malicious email attachments or unsafe downloads.

    Learn more about Ransomware

  • Data Breaches

    Occurs when hackers gain unauthorized access to confidential data, often resulting in privacy violations.
